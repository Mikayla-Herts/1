About Us

Who we are

Our mission is to create a warm, welcoming space at the heart of our local community, where exceptional coffee and tea are served with integrity, thoughtfully sourced from ethical, environmentally responsible producers and paired with lovingly home made pastries and cakes crafted with care. We are committed to sustainability, quality, and connection, offering not just great food and drink but a vibrant local venue where people can gather, host events, and build community, all while supporting both people and planet.
